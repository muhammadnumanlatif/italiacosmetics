Our Brands

Discover the stories behind the world's finest professional beauty brands

Maxylook

Maxylook — Feed Your Hair

Italian excellence in professional haircare. Maxylook harnesses the power of superfoods — Collagen, Macadamia, Argan, Protein, and Fresh Mint — to nourish the hair ecosystem. Every product is a manifestation of health, strength, and beauty. From the protecting shampoos of the Collagen line to the anti-frizz power of Arganway, Maxylook transforms hair care into a ritual of well-being.

Genus

Genus — Professional Haircare Worldwide

One of the most important players in the professional hair market with global distribution coverage. Genus offers targeted solutions through specialized lines: Argan (hydration), Intense Restoring (repair), Hyaluronic Acid (color protection), Keratin (restructuring), Milk (nourishment), and Energy (vitality). A reference point in the cosmetic sector internationally.

Versum

Versum Hair 2.0 — Science Meets Beauty

Elevate your hair care routine with VERSUM HAIR. Where science meets beauty to deliver exceptional results. The new line features advanced collections: Hydrator (moisture), Charcoal Detox (purification), Active Bloom (reinforcing), Soft Touch (anti-frizz), R-tech (reconstruction), and age-defying lamellar elixirs. Every product is designed to transform your hair.

UNA

UNA Rolland USA — High-Performance Treatment

UNA hair is dedicated to high-quality, effective, and gentle hair care. Specializing in anti-hair loss systems (Stop Loss), deep repair (Fortify), intense hydration (Hydro-In), and specialty treatments (Garlic Treatment, Coconut Oil Mask, Etnika). Trusted by salons for visible results — every product delivers professional-grade performance you can feel.

Our Story

Italia Cosmetics was founded with a single belief: that everyone deserves access to professional-grade beauty. We partner directly with the world's most respected haircare brands to bring salon-quality products to your home.

Curating Excellence Since 2018

Born from a passion for Italian beauty and professional craftsmanship, Italia Cosmetics set out to bridge the gap between salon-exclusive products and beauty enthusiasts. We believed that the transformative power of professional haircare shouldn't be limited to salon shelves.

Today, we represent four of the most respected names in professional beauty — Maxylook, Genus, Versum, and UNA — serving thousands of satisfied customers across Europe and beyond. Every product in our collection is vetted by our team of beauty professionals for quality, efficacy, and safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all our products from Maxylook, Genus, Versum, and UNA are 100% authentic, imported directly from Italy. We guarantee the highest professional salon-grade quality across all our haircare and skincare lines.

Do you offer wholesale?

Yes, we offer exclusive wholesale pricing for salons, professional stylists, and authorized distributors. Please use our contact form or email us at support@italiacosmetics.com with your business details to inquire.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we exclusively offer Cash on Delivery (COD) to ensure a safe and trustworthy shopping experience. You can comfortably pay the courier directly when your order is delivered to your doorstep.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an SMS and an email with your courier tracking number. You can use this number on the courier's website to monitor your shipment's progress in real time.

How do I know which product is right for my hair?

Our website categorizes products by brand and hair concern. If you need personalized advice, feel free to reach out to our support team via WhatsApp with details about your hair type, and we'll recommend the perfect professional regimen for you.

Returns Policy

Hassle-free returns under certain conditions.

7-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee on all our professional products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

Return Conditions

We accept returns and exchanges strictly if the product is not damaged, unopened, and in its original pristine packaging. Due to the hygienic nature of cosmetic and haircare products, opened items cannot be accepted for return or exchange unless they arrived damaged or defective.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return process, please contact our support team at support@italiacosmetics.com or via WhatsApp at 00923379912300 within 7 days of receiving your order. Provide your order number and photographic evidence if the product was damaged during transit.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed via bank transfer or mobile wallet within 5-7 business days.

Privacy Policy

Your data is secure with us.
